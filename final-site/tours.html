Adventure Tours

Great Valley Tour

  • Length of tour: 2 to 3 hours (dependent on overall speed)
  • Skill Level: 3, "Adventurer"
  • Distance: 40 miles
  • When: Every day!
  • Age: 12 and up
  • Cost: $34.99
  • Location: Valley West, Bozeman MT

Information: Enjoy a ride through Bozeman's beautiful West Valley. The Great Valley Tour showcases two gorgeous lakes and stunning mountain ranges.

Deep Mountain Tour

  • Length: 4 to 5 hours (dependent on overall speed)
  • Skill Level: 5, "The Long Journey"
  • Distance: 60 miles
  • When: Every day!
  • Age: 17 and up
  • Cost: $54.99
  • Location: Palisade Falls (near Bozeman MT)

Information: Traverse steep mountain trails and rocky terrain in this Deep Mountain Tour. The trail snakes around the beautiful Palisade Falls, occasionally showering riders with cool mist.

Skill Level Rankings:

1, "Meditation" 1hr or less

2, "A Jaunt Through Nature" 2hrs or less

3, "Adventurer" 3hrs or less

4, "Voyager" 4hrs or less

5, "The Long Journey" 5hrs or less

6, "All Out!" 5.5hrs or more
