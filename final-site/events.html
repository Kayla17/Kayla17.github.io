Events

Cycle the Continental Divide

  • Length: 5 to 8 hours (dependent on overall speed)
  • Skill Level: 6, "All Out!"
  • Distance: 100 miles
  • When: August 10, 11, and 13, 2018 (starts at 6:15 a.m.)
  • Requirements: Some biking experience
  • Age: 18 and up
  • Cost: $59.99 (per day)
  • Location: Gallatin National Forest (near Bozeman MT)

Information: The Mountain Spoke's annual event, The Continental Divide, will be held at Gallatin National Forest this year. Bikers will experience the breathtaking beauty of Gallatin National Forest as they travel through lush woods, near deep canyons, up mountains, and around streams and waterfalls. Prepare for steep mountain trails, rugged paths, and windy roads.
