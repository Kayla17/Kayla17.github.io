Record Thunderstorms

The City of Greenville has seen a record high of thunderstorms this spring. With 3 storms in March, 6 in April, and 4 in May, it is speculated that more are incoming. “Reports show 2 storms on their way to Greenville,” weather specialist, Laura Wells said this last Friday, “and more are sure to follow.” Last year’s records documented 8 thunderstorms for Greenville, 5 less than this year’s current number. “I don’t particularly mind,” says local resident, Randel Lewis. “My yard and garden are loving the extra rain.” Local rancher, Paul Evens, however, is not so pleased. “These storms are just terrible,” he says, “Scares my livestock something awful.” With more thunderstorms on the way, the people of Greenville have little choice but to wait them out in their own way.
